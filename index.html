About Me

AI researcher specializing in deep learning solutions and decision support systems for medical diagnosis, with particular expertise in cardiovascular conditions. Experienced in integrating medical domain knowledge into neural network architectures to enhance interpretability and diagnostic accuracy. Passionate about developing multi-modal networks to process and analyze diverse data sources and optimizing algorithms for edge device deployment. Combines technical expertise in machine learning frameworks with a focused approach to translating complex algorithms into practical healthcare applications, bridging the gap between theoretical AI advancement and real-world medical implementation.

Research Focus

Knowledge-aware Deep Learning

Developing knowledge-aware deep learning frameworks that integrate domain expertise and structured knowledge into neural network training, enhancing model performance and interpretability beyond purely data-driven approaches.

AI in medicine

Advancing AI-powered clinical decision support systems through automated analysis of medical signals, imaging data, and patient records to improve healthcare delivery, particularly for cardiovascular diseases.

Edge AI

Developing efficient AI model compression and optimization techniques for edge deployment, enabling intelligent applications on resource-limited hardware platforms.
